ABOUT


Our system combines both hardware and software components to accurately determine the amount of waste inside.This system eliminates the need for manual inspection of bins, reducing the risk of exposure to hazardous waste materials for waste collectors. Additionally, the system can track and segregate waste patterns over time, allowing for more efficient waste management practices. Overall, this system provides a cost-effective and environmentally friendly solution for waste identification and management.
